Hepta Slab is a slab-serif revival based on specimens of
antique genre types from Bruce and Co., primarily Antique
307. The family is a variable font which consists of 10
weights with the extremes intended for display use and
the middle weights for setting text.

BODY COPY

Mr. Fox is the epitome of charm wrapped in fur, sporting a sly grin and a knack for getting into trouble. A self-proclaimed "wild animal," he can't resist a good heist, especially if it involves pilfering from the local farmers. Despite his dashing antics, he’s a softie at heart—just ask Mrs. Fox, who rolls her eyes as he conjures up yet another harebrained scheme while balancing a family dinner on the other paw.

Speaking of Mrs. Fox, she’s the true glue holding this feisty family together. With a patience that rivals that of a saint, she supports Mr. Fox’s shenanigans but isn’t afraid to remind him that they’ve got kids to feed—specifically, an overly dramatic son named Ash. She’s like the wise oracle in a fur coat, always one step ahead and perpetually armed with a rolling pin, ready to whack some sense into her husband when necessary.

Ah, Ash—the teenage fox with a flair for the theatrical and a hairdo that defies gravity. He’s constantly grappling with his dad’s legacy while trying to figure out if he’s more of a daring rebel or a misunderstood artist. Spoiler alert: he’s a bit of both. With his penchant for melodrama, you can count on him to turn every minor setback into an Oscar-worthy performance. Just don’t ask him to help with the family heists; he’s still working on his “cool” factor.

Then there are the bumbling farmers, Boggis, Bunce, and Bean—each a caricature of gluttony, greed, and questionable fashion choices. They’re like a bad reality show waiting to happen, constantly plotting to outsmart Mr. Fox while simultaneously managing to trip over their own feet. Their absurd antics provide the perfect comedic foil to Mr. Fox’s clever schemes, proving that while brains may outsmart brawn, a well-timed pratfall can level the playing field.

Fantastic Feast in the Forrest

Saturday, November 15, 2024

5:00-8:00pm

Local Botanical Garden

Join us for a whimsical outdoor dining experience inspired by the culinary delights of Mr. Fox’s world. Guests will enjoy a farm-to-table feast featuring gourmet versions of classic rustic dishes, including roasted vegetables, apple cider, and delectable pastries. Dress in your best woodland attire, and wander through the garden as we host fun activities like a scavenger hunt and storytelling corners where local actors will recount the adventures of Mr. Fox and his friends.

Daring Fox Adventure

Sunday, December 8, 2024

10:00am-1:00pm

Bring your family and friends for a fun-filled scavenger hunt through the park! Participants will be divided into teams and given clues that lead to various locations inspired by “Fantastic Mr. Fox.” Each stop will feature activities and challenges related to the film, such as “Outsmart the Farmers” obstacle courses and “Fox Trivia” stations. Prizes will be awarded to the team that gathers the most “stolen” treasures by the end!

Whimsical Woodland Film Fest

Saturday, January 25, 2025

7:00-10:00pm

Communinity Outdoor Theater

Join us for a magical evening at the Whimsical Woodland Film Fest featuring a special outdoor screening of "Fantastic Mr. Fox." Bring your blankets and settle in for a cozy night under the stars, complete with popcorn and treats from local vendors. Pre-show festivities include a fox-themed trivia contest and a craft station where kids can create their own fox ears. Enjoy live music inspired by the film’s whimsical spirit before the movie begins, making it a charming night for families and fans alike!
